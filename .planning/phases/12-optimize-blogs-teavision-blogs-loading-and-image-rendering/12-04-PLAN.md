The phase goal explicitly includes "preserving Phase 11 Tea Journal behavior and design," so neither can ship as-is.

Purpose:
- CR-01: The Phase 12 refactor dropped Phase 11's `isFiltered ? filteredArticles : filteredArticles.filter(...)` ternary and now excludes featured articles unconditionally on the filtered (tag/search) path. Featured articles are invisible on every tag page and every `?q=` search; a query matching only featured articles renders EmptyState ("0 articles"). Fix restores Phase 11 semantics: featured articles are only excluded from the main grid on the unfiltered default listing (where they render in FeaturedArticles); on filtered paths they appear like any other article.
- CR-02: The new light `defaultBlogListingQuery` dereferences `featuredPosts[]->{...}` with no publish/slug guard, unlike every other article query in the file. The heavy `getBlog()` path was protected by `getFeaturedArticles()` in-memory intersection; `getDefaultBlogListing()` bypasses that and renders `rawFeatured.slice(0, 2)` directly — leaking scheduled (embargoed) posts publicly and producing guaranteed-404 links for scheduled/slug-less posts.

Output: Two surgical source fixes — drop the featured-exclusion (and now-dead computation/import) on the filtered path in listing-content.tsx; add a `[defined(slug) && publishedAt <= now()]` post-projection filter to the featuredPosts dereference in defaultBlogListingQuery.

Scope discipline: This plan fixes ONLY the two blockers (CR-01, CR-02). The advisory warnings WR-01..WR-05 from 12-VERIFICATION.md (pagination clamping, featured-beyond-two, type assertions, reading time, findTagBySlug URIError) are explicitly OUT OF SCOPE and must NOT be touched — they are not the gate. Do not alter operations.ts; the CR-02 fix is achievable entirely in the GROQ query (the unguarded data simply stops arriving at `rawFeatured`).
