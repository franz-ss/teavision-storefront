<threat_model>

## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Shopify page content to code-owned policy routes | Legal launch availability must not depend on Shopify page records |
| Legacy policy URL to canonical headless URL | Redirects consolidate duplicate policy URLs before App Router page resolution |
| Draft legal wording to visitor trust | Pending owner/legal review must be visible so draft routes are not presented as final legal promises |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-16-01 | Spoofing / Repudiation | legal policy pages | mitigate | Central registry, visible pending-review banners, and approval matrix with proof columns |
| T-16-02 | Tampering / SEO integrity | legacy policy redirects | mitigate | Permanent redirects from legacy `/policies/*` and HTML aliases to canonical `/pages/*` URLs |
| T-16-03 | Information Disclosure / Compliance | policy wording | avoid | Do not invent final legal promises; mark pending pages and route unresolved approval to owner/legal evidence |

</threat_model>
